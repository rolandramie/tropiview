## ✨ Features

### 🛒 Storefront
- **Product Catalog** – Browse products with categories
- **Search & Filter** – Find products instantly
- **Shopping Cart** – Add, remove, adjust quantities
- **Wishlist** – Save favorite items
- **Checkout** – Guest checkout with terms acceptance
- **Customer Reviews** – View product ratings

### 🔐 Admin Dashboard
- **Product Management** – Add, edit, delete, duplicate, copy-paste products
- **Category Management** – Create and organize product categories
- **Order Management** – View and process customer orders
- **Worker Management** – Add, revoke, or delete workers with granular permissions
- **Ad Campaign Management** – Add, edit, delete ads with background style and size selection
- **Banner Management** – Auto-sliding banner rotator (configurable speed)
- **Message Management** – View and reply to customer messages
- **FAQ Management** – Create and manage frequently asked questions
- **Financial Reports** – View revenue, orders, and generate tax reports
- **Activity Log** – Full audit trail of all actions
- **Store Settings** – Configure everything from one panel

### 👥 Worker Dashboard
- **Product Management** – Limited product access (if permitted)
- **Order Processing** – View and complete orders
- **Banner Management** – Add/remove banners (if permitted)
- **Message Management** – Reply to messages (if permitted)
- **Ad Management** – Add/remove ads (if permitted)
- **Activity Log** – Personal action history

### 📢 Advertising
- **Ad Gallery** – Responsive grid layout on homepage
- **Background Styles** – 5 gradient options
- **Size Options** – Main (large) or Small
- **Tag Support** – Highlight special offers
- **Full CRUD** – Create, read, update, delete

### 🔄 Banner Rotator
- Auto-slides every 5 seconds (configurable)
- Custom titles, subtitles, emojis
- Background color options
- Appears on ALL pages
- Dot navigation indicators

### 💬 Messaging
- Customer messages inbox
- One-click reply button
- Threaded conversations
- Worker reply permissions

### 📋 Copy & Paste Products
- Import products from other stores
- JSON format support
- Auto-category detection
- Bulk import ready

### ⚙️ Configuration
- Admin email & password
- Store name & currency
- PayPal business email
- Worker permissions (granular)
- Customer controls (registration, guest checkout)
- Banner speed control
- Terms & conditions editor
- Store open/closed toggle
